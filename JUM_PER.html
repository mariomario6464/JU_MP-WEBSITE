About JU_MP

JU_MP is a platformer game where you control a character with the power to teleport. Navigate challenging levels, avoid obstacles, and discover secrets! Built originally in Scratch, JU_MP is gearing up for a full release in GameMaker.

Features

  • Teleportation mechanic with collision checks and limits
  • Custom animations, sounds, and music
  • Props and polish for a groovy retro feel
  • Playable demo online now!
